I find motivation in forming the link between reliable, elegant technical solutions and putting useful real-world software in the hands of key decision makers.

Guiding Philosophy

  1. If it’s easy to do, don’t do it again. Make a computer do it.
  2. Surround yourself with the greats: people, technologies, problems. If you’re intimidated, it’s probably important.
  3. Avoid fluffy language and irrelevant factors. Capable people can smell useless information from miles away.
  4. Look back on your present from your future. Are you proud of how you spent your time?

These are the guiding philosophies behind everything I do and the motivation behind my contributions: blaze the difficult trail, carefully, by meticulously automating the simple tasks and rigorously justifying the complex ones. This method has provided me with a steadfast foundation for all my successes, from serving as an astrophysical consultant to astronauts and discovering other worlds in space, to delivering robust, cutting-edge ML solutions to the warfighter. All the while, I seek to make my presence an asset and my absence manageable.

I enjoy discussing and solving complex issues. Reach out to me if you want to discuss the universe at cosmological scales; why “artificial intelligence” falls far short of “intelligence,” yet still has considerable utility in today's world; or how to ethically train your pet dog to walk backwards.

Selected Projects

Exohammer

A flexible object-oriented python library that discovers and characterize extrasolar planet by implementing time series analysis via bayesian statistical methods on Doppler and transit timing variation measurements
  • Dependencies include numpy, matplotlib, pickle, easygui, datetime, os, emcee, astropy, ttvfast
  • Generates easily interpreted plots and text files at user-defined intervals, serializes results for further analysis

Pandastrack

A flexible, object-oriented python library for configuring, assessing, and deploying trackers.
  • Leverages pandas accessor for assessment/analytics, a Java interface for live global scale data handling, and Helm charts for deployment flexibility.
  • Used across all General Atomics-i3 portfolios for tracking and knowledge base development and deployment
  • Includes version control and Artifactory deployment in CI/CD for PIP installation
